Logo Usage: Clear Space
The minimum clear space is defined as x, measured by the width of the type stroke or the space between the word “Linked” and the “in” box. The minimum clear space should always be applied. Try to maximize clear space whenever possible.

“in” Box Clear Space
The minimum clear space should always be applied. Try to maximize clear space whenever possible. When the “in” box does not have the ® symbol a smaller clear space can be used. The “in” box does not have to appear with the ® symbol if it is already shown at least once in the document/page.


Minimum Size
The minimum size is 0.25in or 6.35mm, measured by the height of the “in” box. In exceptional circumstances, smaller sizes may be necessary. (ex. on a pen) In such cases, legibility should always be your top priority. ® symbol minimum size to be used with the logo is .3125in or 7.9375mm, measured by the height of the “in” box.


The smallest icon size is 16x16 pixels, and it reserved for our favicon.
